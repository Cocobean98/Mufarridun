The Story Behind Mufarridun

One project for my Dunya, one for my Akhira.

Assalamu Alaikum. I'm a 28-year-old engineer, and I'm the sole provider for my family of six. Like many, I've been navigating the heavy realities of low wages and financial strain, constantly searching for a way to build a stable future. I've built two AI tools: one is a premium subscription service for my dunya (worldly life), and the other is ReelDeen — a completely free project for my Akhira (hereafter).

My dream is simple but heavy on my heart: to one day buy a home, get married, and achieve the financial independence that has always felt just out of reach. I've knocked on many doors, but I am waiting to walk through the one where Allah has placed my rizq.

I made a vow to Allah: if He grants me financial stability through this project, I will take Kafalah (sponsor the education of an orphan), give generously to the poor, support my friends, and most importantly, continue to provide for my family with dignity.

If you're reading this, thank you from the bottom of my heart. May Allah increase you in rizq and barakah.
